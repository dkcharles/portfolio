Research

Research Areas and Themes.

The research portfolio connects core artificial intelligence methods with interactive media, connected health, virtual production, and translational collaboration.

Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning

Work in AI and machine learning spans unsupervised learning, pattern recognition, intelligent systems, and applied analytics across multiple domains.

Games, Narrative, and Interactive Systems

Research in games includes player modelling, game design, dynamic multi-player worlds, intelligent game agents, and interactive storytelling systems.

XR, Rehabilitation, and Connected Health

Health-related research includes virtual reality rehabilitation, brain-computer interfaces, game-based rehabilitation, and immersive technologies for patient support.

Creative and Translational Innovation

Recent work extends into virtual production, AI-assisted animation, creative technology networks, and collaborative innovation with external partners and funders.
